Return Policy

Customers may return eligible products within the return window if the item is unused, defective, damaged, incorrect, or not as described.

Return approval depends on:
- Return window status.
- Product condition.
- Order status.
- Customer claim history.
- Supporting evidence.

Returns are generally accepted when:
- The claim is submitted within the valid return window.
- The customer provides a valid reason.
- The item is damaged, defective, incorrect, or not as described.
- The order metadata confirms the purchase and delivery.

Returns may require manual review when:
- The return window has expired.
- The product is high value.
- The customer has frequent previous claims.
- The evidence does not clearly support the complaint.
