Damaged Delivery Policy

A damaged delivery claim applies when a product or package arrives broken, crushed, torn, leaking, dented, or visibly mishandled during shipping.

The claim is stronger when:
- The customer mentions damaged packaging.
- The uploaded image shows product damage, crushed packaging, broken parts, or missing components.
- The order was recently delivered.
- The complaint is submitted within the return or replacement window.

Recommended action:
- If damage is visible and the customer requests replacement, approve replacement when the order is eligible.
- If damage is visible and the customer requests refund, approve refund when the order is eligible.
- If damage evidence is unclear, send the claim to manual review.
- If the order is high value or the customer has high claim risk, send the claim to manual review.

Escalate when:
- The customer sentiment is highly negative.
- The issue is urgent.
- The product is expensive.
- Multiple items in the order are damaged.
