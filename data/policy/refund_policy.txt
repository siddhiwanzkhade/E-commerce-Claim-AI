Refund Policy

Customers may be eligible for a refund when the product is damaged, missing, incorrect, or not delivered within the expected delivery window.

Refunds are generally approved when:
- The customer reports the issue within the valid return/refund window.
- The order status confirms the item was delivered, delayed, missing, or not delivered.
- The customer provides clear evidence such as product images, package images, or delivery details.
- The customer has low or medium claim risk.

Refunds may require manual review when:
- The order value is high.
- The customer has submitted multiple previous claims.
- The image evidence is unclear or does not match the complaint.
- The customer is outside the return/refund window.
- The delivery status conflicts with the customer complaint.

Refunds should be rejected or escalated when:
- The claim appears fraudulent.
- The product damage is not visible and no supporting evidence is provided.
- The claim is made far outside the allowed policy window without valid reason.
