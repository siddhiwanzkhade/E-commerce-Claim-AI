Replacement Policy

Customers may be eligible for a replacement when the product arrives damaged, defective, broken, missing parts, or incorrect.

Replacement is recommended when:
- The complaint indicates a damaged, defective, broken, or incorrect item.
- The customer requests a replacement.
- The product image confirms visible damage or a missing/broken component.
- The order is within the replacement window.
- The customer has low previous-claim risk.

Replacement may require manual review when:
- The image evidence is unclear.
- The order value is high.
- The customer has medium or high claim history.
- The product is outside the replacement window.
- The damage appears caused by customer misuse rather than delivery or product defect.

If the product is unavailable, the claim may be converted to a refund or escalation.
